Research

Our lab studies human visual perception and temporal processing,
with a particular focus on motion perception, perceptual organization,
and image-computable / deep-learning models of human vision.

We combine psychophysical experiments (continuous tracking, segmentation tasks,
motion perception tasks) with computational modeling.
Experiments are run in a controlled visual environment using calibrated displays
and high-performance computing resources for large-scale simulations and training.

Research environment

▶ Wearable eye tracking and video
Tobii Pro Glasses 3 system Wearable eye tracking

Wearable eye-tracking system for natural behavior, social interaction, and mobile visual-attention studies. The lab set includes the eye-tracking unit, corrective lenses, an external processing unit, and accessories for participant setup and recording workflows.

Tobii Glasses 2 system Wearable eye tracking

Earlier-generation wearable eye tracker for mobile gaze and behavior recordings. The lab set includes the wearable eye tracker and accessory box.

Pivothead video recording eyewear First-person video

Wearable video eyewear for first-person scene recording and behavior observation.

▶ Screen-based eye tracking
Tobii Pro X3-120 120 Hz screen-based eye tracker

Compact screen-based eye tracker for controlled visual attention and psychophysics experiments.

Tobii X2-30 Compact with mobile-device stand Compact screen-based eye tracking

Compact eye tracker for gaze measurement on laptop, monitor, or mobile-device setups. The lab set includes the X2-30 tracker and a mobile-device stand.

Tobii Pro Nano Portable screen-based eye tracker

Portable research eye tracker for screen-based attention, decision-making, and interaction studies.

Legacy Tobii screen-based trackers EyeX, Eye Tracker 4C, and T60

Legacy Tobii devices retained for gaze-interaction prototypes, demonstrations, compatibility checks, and screen-based experiment setups.

▶ Physiology, VR, and computing
NeXus 10 MKII Multimodal physiology recording

Multimodal physiology system for EEG, EMG, heart rate, skin conductance, respiration, and related signals.

FOVE eye-tracking VR headset Eye-tracking virtual reality

VR headset with integrated eye tracking for immersive gaze-based research.

MSI Creator M16 laptop Experiment and modeling workstation

High-performance laptop for experiment control, data processing, and model development.

SHARP SH930W AQUOS PHONE Mobile display and device-control testing

Android smartphone for mobile display, app-based, or device-control experiments.

▶ Accessories and connectivity
Calibrite Display Pro HL High-luminance display calibration

Professional display calibration device for color profiling LCD, mini-LED, OLED, and Apple XDR panels. The HL sensor measures up to 3,000 nits and supports accurate monitor calibration workflows with Calibrite PROFILER software.

Battery charging and network adapters Experiment support accessories

Support equipment for field sessions, device charging, stable wired data transfer, and device connectivity. The lab set includes an HXY-H4 battery charger and a USB Type-C to Gigabit Ethernet adapter.
