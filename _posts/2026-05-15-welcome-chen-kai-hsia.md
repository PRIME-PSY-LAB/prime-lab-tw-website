We are happy to welcome Chen-Kai Hsia to PRIME Lab. Chen-Kai is an undergraduate student in the Department of Psychology at Soochow University, and he is interested in eye-tracking research and gaming-related studies.

In the lab, he will begin exploring how visual attention, interaction, and game contexts can connect with perceptual psychology. We look forward to seeing him bring his curiosity and enthusiasm into PRIME Lab, and we hope this will be a place where he can fully develop his interests.
